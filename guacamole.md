| **Ingredients:** |
- 2 avocados 
- 1 lime 
- 2 tsp salt 
- 1 onion 
- 1 tomato

| **Instructions:** |
- prepare a bowl for the guacamole 
- cut and mash avocados, add to bowl 
- chop both the onion and tomato, add and mix into the bowl of avocados
- squeeze lime in the mixture
- add salt
- and mix well one last time 
- enjoy!
